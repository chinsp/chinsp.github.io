About me

I am a doctoral researcher at the deparment of mathematics, Karlsruhe Institute for Technology (KIT), Germany and am funded by the CRC-1173 on the project B9 with Prof. Dr. Martin Frank. My research interests lie in the application and implementation of memory and computationally efficient methods for kinetic equations. These kinetic equations are of interest, for instance, when considering at supernova explosions, star formations, fusion reactions and more. I combine solving these high-dimensional kinetic equations with finding memory efficient low-rank solutions and quantifying uncertainty in solutions.
